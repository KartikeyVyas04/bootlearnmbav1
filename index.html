Tips to Score 99+ Percentile in CAT

üéØ Key: High accuracy and smart selection matter more than number of attempts.

1. Master Time Management

  • Scan quickly for easiest questions first.
  • Skip lengthy calculation questions‚Äîcome back later.
  • Take regular timed sectionals and full-length mocks.

2. Section Strategies

VARC: Focus on RCs; read questions before passages.
DILR: Identify easier sets and attempt TITA first.
QA: Master Arithmetic, use shortcuts, and practice mental math.

3. Practice Smart Mock Taking

  • Analyze all solutions after mocks.
  • Log errors and create personal revision sheets.
  • Simulate exam environment: morning, noon, and evening slots.

4. Maximize Accuracy

  • Aim for >75% accuracy; avoid wild guesses on MCQs.
  • TITA has no negative marking, so try to solve more.

5. Build Strong Fundamentals

  • Master basics before advanced topics; revise formulae regularly.
  • Read diverse opinions/editorials for VARC growth.

6. Attempt Selection

  • Choose questions you're most confident in.
  • Don't attempt all, but maximize marks with selective answering.
